About Me

Hi, I’m Jimmy — an engineering student who used to struggle with maths. What changed? A few great teachers who explained things clearly and believed in me. Now I want to pass that on. I started Jimmy’s Math Tutoring to help students feel confident, not confused. If I can learn to love maths, anyone can.
